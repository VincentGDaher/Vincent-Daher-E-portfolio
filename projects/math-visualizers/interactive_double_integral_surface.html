1. The base

The rectangle on the xy-plane is the region R. Its area is (b − a)(d − c).

2. The height

At every point of R, the function gives a height z = f(x,y).

3. The integral

The program adds many tiny columns: height × tiny area. This approximates the volume under the surface.
